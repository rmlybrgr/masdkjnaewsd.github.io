About Us

Hi-Tech Company is one of the leading electronic gadget resellers in Malaysia. It was founded by a group of students who graduated from TAR University College in 2020. Our company has 50 retail stores all over the country that sell top-quality electronic gadgets such as laptops, desktops, tablets, smartphones, computer hardware, and other accessories at a reasonable price. Moreover, our company is headquartered in KL. We sell products with a wide range of brands, including but not limited to ASUS, DELL, LENOVO, APPLE, ACER, HP, Samsung, OPPO, Kingston, JBL, and other popular brands. At Hi-Tech, we also provide after-sales services such as repairs. We want to provide our clients with a cosy, at-home experience and let them relax while dealing with us.
